What are the occupations of men and women in the US?

Using the 2021 5-year estimate from Social Explorer, we can see some interesting patterns of occupation for men and women in the US.

Note about the data - although sex is collected as binary data in the Census, it may not accurately represent how many feel about their gender. Additionally, data on occupation is written in and does not reflect multiple jobs and military positions. Moreover, census makes assumptions on incomplete data by looking at "similar persons".
