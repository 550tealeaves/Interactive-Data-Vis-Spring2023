Where do people work?

Types of occupation for men and women according to the US Census

What is an occupation?

The Census defines an occupation as something that "describes the kind of work the person does on the job"

Why is this information important?

Among the reasons the Census collects occupation data includes to

  • Inform policy programs for employment, career development & training, and to assess anti-discrimination
  • Calculate state per capita income estimates
  • Create jobs because companies can see where to expand
