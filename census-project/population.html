How many people are theoretically able to work?

In full disclosure, the census determines occupation for individuals 16 and up. However, their age categories include 15-17; therefore, this bar chart includes teenagers to young to work. Furthermore, this is just the total number of people who ideally could have an occupation and does not include things like those unemployed, or unable to work. I kept the population 85 and up because according to the Bureau of Labor Statistics, the labor force of workers over 75 is projected to increase 96.5% from 2020-2030.

Note about the data - although sex is collected as binary data in the Census, it may not accurately represent how many individuals feel about their gender.
